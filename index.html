Welcome to Fit in IQHSB

Your personalized tool for fitness and wellness at IQanat High School of Burabay. This app helps you create and track your training and nutrition plans based on your personal goals and the school's facilities.

To get started, please navigate to the **Profile** page to set up your information.

About the School

IQanat High School of Burabay is a boarding Cambridge A-level school located near the beautiful Burabay National Park. The school is committed to the holistic development of its students, providing state-of-the-art sports facilities including gymnasiums, football and basketball courts, a climbing wall, and zones for winter sports. This application is designed to help you make the most of these resources to achieve your health and fitness goals.

Important Safety Disclaimer

This tool is for informational and planning purposes only and is not a substitute for professional medical or fitness advice. Students must seek approval from a school coach or medical staff member before beginning any training or nutrition plan. All plans generated by this application should be reviewed by a professional to ensure they are safe and appropriate for your individual needs and health status.
